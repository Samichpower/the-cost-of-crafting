GAME RULES

Singleplayer

  • The Cost of Crafting is simple!
  • The game will consist of 8 rounds, each getting progressively more difficult.
  • You will have 60 seconds to answer as many questions as possible
  • Each round, I will name 1 Minecraft item.
  • Your job is to tell me, THE QUANTITY OF EACH INGREDIENT needed to craft that item in a CRAFTING TABLE.
  • The crafting grid positioning is not important.
  • If your answer is correct, you will win a diamond.
  • But if you get ANY answer wrong, your game is immediately over.
  • Answering all 8 questions correctly will earn you a bonus diamond.
  • The game ends when the clock expires, or if you answer incorrectly.
  • Good Luck! The timer will start after I name the first item!

Multiplayer

  • The Cost of Crafting is simple!
  • The game will consist of 8 rounds, each getting progressively more difficult.
  • Each round, I will name 1 item in Mineccraft.
  • Your job is to tell me, ONLY THE QUANTITY OF EACH INGREDIENT needed to craft that item in a crafting table.
  • Crafting grid positioning is not important.
  • The first person to press their button has the chance to answer.
  • If they answer correctly, they will win a diamond & the game moves to the next round.
  • If they answer incorrectly, they are eliminated from play, and the next player to press their button will have the chance to answer.
  • But if you get ANY answer wrong, you will be eliminated and your game is over.
  • The game will continue until all players are eliminated, or all 8 rounds expire.
  • Good Luck!
